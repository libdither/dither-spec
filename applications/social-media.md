# Social Media

Why is social media?
 - People want to share things to certain people.
 - Other people want to consume different kinds of things at different times.
 - Conversation is just people wanting to read immediately what someone else has written.

How is social media?
 - Things get posted. Sometimes restrictions on who should be able to read.
 - Need to model / characterize what gets posted.
 - People need to somehow communicate what they want to see. Computer should predict what people want to see. Take into account psychology, long-term well-being. Search space of all posts, compile for user.
